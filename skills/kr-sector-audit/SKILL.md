# Korean Sector Audit

Use this skill to review an existing Korea sector document.

## Quick Start

- Start from the actual memo or report.
- Focus on factual support, internal consistency, date discipline, and unsupported inference.
- Lead with findings, ordered by severity.
- Do not silently rewrite the document. Explain what is wrong and why.

## Workflow

1. Read the document.
   Identify the intended scope, the claimed numbers, and the source style.
2. Check support.
   Look for missing citations, stale dates, mixed-date metrics, and unsupported claims.
3. Check structure.
   Look for missing critical sections, especially sector definition, risks, listed exposure mapping, and source disclosure.
4. Write findings-first.
   Prioritize bugs in reasoning and factual integrity over style comments.

## Operating Rules

- Findings come first.
- Acknowledge when no material issues are found.
- Residual risks and testing gaps still belong in the audit even when findings are light.
