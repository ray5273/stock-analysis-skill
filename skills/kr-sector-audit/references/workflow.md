# Workflow Reference

## Audit Priorities

Prioritize these in order:

1. unsupported or contradictory numbers
2. missing or stale source attribution
3. mixed-date comparisons presented as one snapshot
4. sector-definition ambiguity
5. missing key sections
6. overconfident inference from thin evidence

## Checklist

- Are the market figures source-backed?
- Are dates present where they matter?
- Are Korea-only and global figures clearly distinguished?
- Are listed-company exposures labeled correctly?
- Are constraints and risks treated seriously, or is the memo one-sided?
- Does the source list actually support the claims made above it?

## Failure Modes To Avoid

- spending the audit on wording instead of factual integrity
- treating an undated marketing page as a current market statistic
- missing that the document compares different dates as if they were one period
