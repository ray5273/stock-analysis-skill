# Korean Market Checklists

Use these lists after completing the base workflow. Keep the analysis tied to current DART filings, KRX disclosures, company IR materials, and market data.

## KRX Growth Stock Checklist

### Unit Economics And Reinvestment

- Check whether growth is still purchased with margin sacrifice or is now compounding with operating leverage.
- Check whether recurring revenue, backlog, or retention style signals exist and are actually disclosed.
- Check whether growth depends on one-off projects, policy support, or unsustainably high capex.
- Check whether valuation already assumes margin normalization and long runway.

### Expectation Risk

- Compare current valuation to the durability of the growth story.
- Check whether management has a record of hitting or missing growth promises.
- Check whether recent price strength already discounts near-term beats.

## KRX Value Stock Checklist

### Earnings Quality

- Check whether earnings are cyclical peak, depressed trough, or normalized base.
- Check whether cheapness comes from governance discount, capital misallocation, or structural decline.
- Check whether free cash flow conversion supports the apparent value.

### Discount Persistence

- Check why the stock is cheap and what would actually close the gap.
- Check whether treasury shares, dividends, buybacks, or cancellations support rerating.
- Check whether related-party issues or capital allocation history argue against rerating.

## Exporter Checklist

### End-Market Exposure

- Identify which end markets actually drive demand.
- Check customer concentration and whether volume depends on one large buyer or a narrow supply chain.
- Check whether current demand is cyclical, policy-driven, or replacement driven.

### FX And Margin Sensitivity

- Check transaction currency and cost base.
- Check whether margin improves or worsens with FX moves.
- Check whether working-capital swings make reported earnings less reliable.

## Holding Company And Restructuring Checklist

### Look-Through Value

- Estimate look-through NAV and note key assumptions.
- Check whether major subsidiaries trade at high or low valuations versus history.
- Check whether treasury shares, cross-holdings, or non-core assets distort the market value.

### Discount Persistence

- Check board, ownership, and related-party governance because discount persistence is often a governance issue, not only a valuation issue.
- Check whether there is a realistic catalyst such as cancellation, spin-off, sale, or shareholder-return change.
- Check whether minority holders have a believable path to value realization.
